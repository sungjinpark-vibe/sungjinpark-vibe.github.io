LoL Player Type

Behavioral Archetype Research · non-commercial academic project

A research project that studies the behavioral patterns of League of Legends laners across regions and ranked tiers, using the official Riot Games API. This site is the product page for our Riot API key application.

Goals

  • Group laner play styles into a small set of behavioral archetypes via unsupervised clustering.
  • Measure how archetypes shift across tiers (Iron → Challenger).
  • Measure how they shift across regions (KR, NA, EU, LATAM, …).

Data

  • ~300 laners per role, per tier, per region.
  • Aggregated Ranked Solo/Duo (queue 420) metrics from the 2025 competitive splits (Split 1 & 2).
  • APIs: LEAGUE-V4, MATCH-V5, SUMMONER-V4.

How it helps players & League of Legends

By showing the concrete behavioral patterns that distinguish higher tiers — per role and region — the study aims to give players measurable paths to improve and to provide a policy-compliant resource for coaching, meta understanding, and balance/matchmaking research.

Privacy & compliance

Results are reported only in aggregate. PUUIDs are used transiently and never published; no raw player data is exposed.
